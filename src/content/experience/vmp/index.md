# User Experience Designer
**VMP International · Automated Retail** · February 2010–February 2014

As a key member at VMP, a trailblazing startup in ATM solutions, I was instrumental in shaping a unique B2C platform tailored for automated sales centers.

My role was multi-dimensional, encompassing the orchestration of the product's entire lifecycle. I was actively involved in strategizing the product's future roadmap and meticulously overseeing its continuous development.

My responsibilities extended to drafting comprehensive specification documents including MRD and PRD, formulating user stories, and conducting product demonstrations. A significant part of my role involved crafting a user experience marked by fluidity and innovation, evident in our wireframes and user interfaces. I also led a diverse team of freelancers, ensuring their contributions aligned seamlessly with our development objectives.
